Executive Profile

A Visionary Leader in Real Estate & Corporate Development

With over 15 years of experience in real estate development and corporate leadership, Dr. Puneet Aggarwal is the visionary force behind Grand Sapphire Infratech Private Limited (GSIPL). His journey reflects a deep commitment to excellence, integrity, and long-term value creation in India's dynamic property and infrastructure sector.

Dr. Aggarwal believes that real estate is more than building structures — it is about shaping communities and delivering trust. Under his leadership, GSIPL has developed and promoted residential townships, commercial hubs, and integrated urban projects designed to enhance modern lifestyles while maintaining strong investment fundamentals.

From land acquisition and strategic planning to project execution and customer delivery, he has consistently focused on quality, transparency, and sustainable growth.

"We don't just develop properties — we create destinations where aspirations take shape."

— Dr. Puneet Aggarwal
