About

Visionary Leadership

With over 15 years of experience in real estate development and corporate leadership, Puneet Aggarwal is the visionary force behind Grand Sapphire Infratech Private Limited (GSIPL). His journey reflects a deep commitment to excellence, integrity, and long-term value creation in India's dynamic property and infrastructure sector.

"We don't just develop properties — we create destinations where aspirations take shape."
— Puneet Aggarwal

A Vision Beyond Construction

Puneet Aggarwal believes that real estate is more than building structures — it is about shaping communities and delivering trust. Under his leadership, GSIPL has developed and promoted residential townships, commercial hubs, and integrated urban projects designed to enhance modern lifestyles while maintaining strong investment fundamentals.

Leadership & Corporate Governance

Beyond his flagship company, Puneet Aggarwal has served in leadership capacities across multiple real estate and infrastructure entities, strengthening his expertise in corporate governance, infrastructure planning, strategic expansion, and investment-backed asset creation.

Expanding Horizons

With initiatives like Desi Fasal, he demonstrates a forward-looking approach to business, focusing on diversified, asset-backed investment models that bridge urban development with sustainable agricultural ventures.
